VikasDairy

Procurement

 



Vikas Dairy sells 1000 products per day as of May 31st, 2019 from about 2,20,789 dairy products covering more than 7,598 villages across India. The company is procuring high-quality Products by establishing production collection centers.

Vikas has created cold chain facilities from producer to consumer to ensure the freshness of the Products at all stages. The company has a team of veterinary supporting staff to handled on products production.
