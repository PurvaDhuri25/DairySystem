About Vikas Dairy

Vilkas Dairy Limited is a public limited company in Jalgaon Jilha Sahakari Dudh Utpadak Sangh is a pioneering organization involved in procurement, processing and distribution of milk and milk products in and around Jalgaon district known as Khandesh. 'VIKAS', as it is popularly known, has clear cut goals of uplifting the lot of farmers by giving them their due and making sure that the end user gets best quality milk products at most reasonable prices. In fact,the Sangh has carved out a special niche for itself and its products in the public eye and has become market leader in the region

To supply good and safe milk & milk products consistently through continual improvement of our systems and practices.

Corporate Social Responsibility

A training center has been established for training and orienting the milk producing farmers and rural youth for rearing the best quality Milch animals and managing their own business affairs right from village level to the apex level. Leadership and other Development programmes for rural women are regularly organized. Blood donation camps for agencies like Red Cross etc. are a regular feature. Relief for natural disaster struck areas are always a priority.
